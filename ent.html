SmartBPM Features

SmartBPM: Governed Business Process Automation. Built for Teams.

A configurable forms and workflow engine, deployed as a Microsoft Teams Add-in, running inside your existing M365 tenant.

🏗️

Configurable Forms & Workflows

Design multi-step approval forms without code. Configure routing rules, conditional logic, and role-based access — all through an admin interface.

🔒

Governed. Auditable. Compliant.

Every action logged. Every approval recorded. Full audit trail baked in by design — not added as an afterthought.

🔗

Deep SAP Integration

Surface SAP data directly in Teams forms. Write approved transactions back to SAP automatically. RFC, BAPI, OData — real integration, not connectors.

Secured Inside Your M365 Tenant

No data leaves your Azure AD boundary. Deployed within your existing tenant. Secured by Microsoft, governed by your IT policies.

Works Where Your People Work

Teams desktop, browser, and mobile. Your approvers action requests wherever they are — no new app installs, no new logins.

You Own the Code

SmartBPM is delivered as source code. Your team can maintain, extend, and modify it. No vendor lock-in. No subscription dependency.
